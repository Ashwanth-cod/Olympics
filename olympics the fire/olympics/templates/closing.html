2024 Paris Olympics Closing Ceremony

A Celebration of Diversity and Solidarity


The closing ceremony of the 2024 Summer Olympics took place at Stade de France on 11 August 2024, showcasing cultural presentations and concluding remarks.

The program began on a solemn note, featuring French singer Zaho de Sagazan and a choir singing "Sous le ciel de Paris."

Katie Ledecky and other athletes participated in a vibrant parade of nations, celebrating athletic excellence and unity.

Tom Cruise made a dramatic appearance for the Olympic flag handoff to Los Angeles, showcasing the next host city's excitement.

Performances by Billie Eilish and other renowned artists highlighted the festivities of the closing ceremony, leaving a lasting impression.
